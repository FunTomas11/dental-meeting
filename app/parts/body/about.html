ptss Polish Association of Dental Students (PTSS) is an organization associating students of the medical and dental faculty throughout Poland. The scope of our activities includes scientific activities, organizing training for students and preventive campaigns aimed at educating the public about oral health.

The PTSS branch at the Medical University of Lublin has almost 220 members. We pay particular attention to promoting proper oral hygiene among children and adults, but also to personal education and gaining experience during the workshops we organize. We have already organized our flagship National Scientific Conference Denteast twice - in 2016 and 2020, and each of them was very popular.
